## üí° Study Tips for Week 1

1. **Don't just watch - DO**: Open a spreadsheet while watching tutorials
2. **Type formulas manually**: Don't copy-paste, build muscle memory
3. **Ask "why?"**: Understand why data is organized a certain way
4. **Real data practice**: Use your own data (expenses, workout logs, etc.)
5. **Take notes**: Write down what you learn in your own words
