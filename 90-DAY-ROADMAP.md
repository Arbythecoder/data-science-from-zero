## üìö Learning Approach

### Daily Structure:
1. **Concept** (15 min) - Understand the 'why'
2. **Example** (20 min) - See it in action
3. **Practice** (25 min) - Build it yourself
4. **Reflect** (5 min) - Write what you learned

### Weekly Structure:
- **Mon-Fri**: Learn new concepts
- **Sat**: Mini-project applying the week's skills
- **Sun**: Review, reflect, and rest

## üí° Key Principles

1. **Understanding over memorization** - Know why, not just how
2. **Practice with real data** - No toy examples
3. **Comment everything** - Explain your thinking
4. **Build progressively** - Each day builds on the last
5. **Reflect often** - Writing solidifies learning
